Organizational Profile

Established in 2024, Surf Life Saving Philippines Inc. (SLSP) and the South East Asia Lifesaving (SEAL) Network serve as the lead agencies for drowning prevention, water safety, and professional lifeguard development, operating with a National Office in Bacnotan, La Union.

Our Vision

To become the leading center of excellence in drowning prevention, water safety, lifesaving education, emergency response, and lifesaving sports in the Philippines and Southeast Asia.

Our Mission

To reduce drowning and strengthen community resilience through evidence-based research, professional training, lifesaving sports, and strategic partnerships deploying internationally recognized best practices.

Governance & Professional Mentorship

Our operations are strictly guided by the internationally recognized standards and best practices of the Cudgen Headland Surf Life Saving Club, the Royal Life Saving Society Australia – New South Wales, and the South East Asia Lifesaving (SEAL) Network. To ensure absolute fidelity to global public safety benchmarks, we are privileged to receive ongoing technical guidance and professional mentorship from elite, highly experienced Australian lifesaving professionals and lifesaving sports athletes.

What We Do

1. Drowning Research

Execution of Learning Conferences, WHO ICD-10 epidemiological tracking, policy studies, and academic publications.

2. Strategic Partnership

Establishing powerful collaborative alliances with LGUs, national line agencies, international NGOs, and corporate partners.

3. Nippers & Swim and Survive (Matatag Na Bata)

Grassroots youth water-familiarization, survival swimming literacy, and essential coastal resilience modules.

4. Emergency Care and CPR

Community responder and professional Basic Life Support (BLS) training strictly adhering to the ANZCOR standard.

5. Professional Lifeguarding Training

Standardized open-water, pool, and mechanized rescue capabilities encompassing robust instructor development.

6. Drowning Prevention Symposium

Secondary school dry-land water safety programs deploying the innovative participant "Skills Passport" system.

7. Lifesaving Sports

Annual regional and national lifesaving sports events essential for maintaining physical fitness and tactical readiness.
